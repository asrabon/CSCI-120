Halloween

Come in and celebrate halloween with us and try all of our different spooky pizzas while out trick or treating. Best costume of the night will receive a 100 dollar gift card for our resturant. Candy bags will be handed out to the little ones.

Valentines Day

Come in to celebrate valentine's day with that special someone in your life. Any pizza this day will be made into the shape of a heart. Will have a nice romantic atmosphere featuring candles and a live band.

Chinese New Year

Come join us in celebrating the Chinese New Year! We will be featuring a brand new pizza based on what year it is like year of the chicken. Tickets will be handed out with each purchase that day and if your ticket is drawn at the start of the new year you will have free pizza for a year.
